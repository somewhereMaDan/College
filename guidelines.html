Guidelines For Paper Presentations

  1. • Abstract should be in Times New Roman, font size 12, italicized and not be more than 250 words.
  2. • Full paper should be submitted in MS Word format and should not exceed 3500 words; text should be typed in Times New Roman, font size 12 and all text should be one-and-a half line spaced and fully justified.
  3. • Heading should be bold faced in font size 14, sub heading bold faced and in font size 12.
  4. • Each page should be numbered at the bottom on the right side.
  5. • References should be in the APA format.
  6. • Verifiable sources for the secondary Information are to be given.
  7. • Cover page should contain title of the paper, author/s' name and affiliation, contact details including mobile number and email address.
  8. • If the paper is co-authored by more than one, all the authors need to be registered individualy.
  9. • There will be a video Presentation for the selected papers in the technical sessions. On the final selection, the presenters shall be informed through mail.
